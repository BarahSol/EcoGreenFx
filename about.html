About the Inventor

Dr Uchechi Bliss Onyedikachi is a Senior Lecturer and Deputy Postgraduate Coordinator at Michael Okpara University of Agriculture, Umudike (MOUAU), with extensive expertise in environmental biochemistry and sustainable development. Her research focuses on addressing toxic pollution, particularly in crude oil-contaminated areas like Ogoniland, through innovative green solutions such as bioremediation strategies using plant-microbe synergies. She has actively contributed to science communication, mentoring, and community engagement, impacting public policies and the implementation of large-scale environmental remediation projects.

Dr. Onyedikachi has authored numerous publications in reputable journals and presented her findings at various international conferences. With a Google Scholar citation impact of 254, an H-index of 7, and an i10-index of 6, her work continues to influence academia and policy. She has also established initiatives like her EcoGreenFX green solution, engagements with farmers, and the African Science Frontiers Initiative’s (ASFI) maiden teatime program to foster collaboration among African scientists.

As Vice President of Communication and Public Engagements at ASFI, she leads key initiatives, including the 2024 Bootcamp themed "Revolutionizing Perspectives: Unleashing Innovation in African Research." A passionate mentor, Dr. Onyedikachi runs the "YouToo Can Make It" platform, inspiring women, girls, and young researchers to excel in academia, leadership, and communication.

Dr. Onyedikachi’s vision includes promoting cutting-edge research, digitalization, and international-standard research centers in Africa while advocating for policies that empower women in science. Her leadership roles, impactful mentorship, and dedication to innovation position her as a transformative figure in African science and sustainability.

About EcoGreenFX

EcoGreenFX is an innovative solution designed to address crude oil sludge contamination in soils through a synergistic approach involving co-planting and microbial bioremediation. Developed as part of a UKaid-sponsored Innovation to Market (I2M) program, EcoGreenFX integrates sustainable agricultural practices with environmental remediation techniques to restore soil health and enhance food security.

This novel approach leverages the natural pollutant-degrading properties of plants and microbes, combined with organic amendments like animal manure, to break down hydrocarbons and mitigate environmental pollution. By utilizing locally available resources, EcoGreenFX not only reduces remediation costs but also empowers communities to sustainably manage polluted lands.

With its focus on ecological restoration, EcoGreenFX aligns with global sustainability goals, offering scalable solutions for environmental recovery in oil-contaminated regions while promoting resilient agricultural practices.

The Inspiration Behind EcoGreenFX

The inventor, Dr Uchechi Bliss Onyedikachi, saw the harmful effects of oil contamination on ecosystems and agricultural land. After extensive research and collaboration, EcoGreenFX was developed to harness nature's own resources to address this growing environmental issue.

EcoGreenFX represents a fusion of innovative science, ecological awareness, and sustainability, aimed at transforming the way we restore polluted environments and support resilient farming practices.
